The Best Movies According to Alyssa

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sweeney Todd

This is my favorite musical. I love the dramatic music and images.

Amelie

This movie is entirely in French. I love the quarky characters, the music by Yann Tiersen, and the joy it brings to me each time I watch it.

The Holiday

This is my favorite Rom-Com. The actors do a phenomenal job and I get pulled into the love stories everytime.
